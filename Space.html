Space


Early 2015, whilst studying for my masters I designed and programmed Space in my spare time with my friend Evgeniy Medvedev whilst our friend Alex Hayes drew the art.


The aim of this project was to create something slightly bigger than I had before and further my skills in game development and Unity. The game is a top down shooter, the aim being to increase your score through the destruction of enemies and asteroids with the aid of offensive and defensive powerups. This project used C# and Unity.
