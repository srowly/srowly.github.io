Soul Linked


I designed and programmed Soul Linked alongside my friends Johan Wangsell and Evgeniy Medvedev whilst our friends Alex Hayes and Jacob Torrang drew the art.


This game was created for the 32nd Ludum Dare in March 2015 following the theme "An Unconventional Weapon". To fit this, we made the core mechanic of the game, the players main weapon, the ability to link to enemies. This link transfers any damage the player recieves to the enemy he is linked with, the goal then being to take damage, allowing the player to move through obstacles that would have otherwise harmed him or kill the enemies themselves.This game was created using C# and Unity.
