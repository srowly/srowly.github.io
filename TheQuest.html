The Quest


The Quest is one of the earlier games I programmed. It's a turn based dungeon crawler, each time the player moves, attacks etc, a turn is used, followed by the monsters in the dungeon also taking a turn.


The aim of the game is to kill the monsters on each level. The player can pick up several items, such as potions and weapons, each of which has a different function. For example a bow and arrow hits in a straight line infront of the player, whereas the sword only hits directly infront.
